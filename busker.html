The Brooklyn Busker

PROJECT 01 // MUSICAL PERFORMANCE & ENGAGEMENT

Street performance is the ultimate study in public engagement. As the "Brooklyn Busker," I bring live violin performance to the public squares of New York and the benches of Central Park.

Balancing the technical precision required in Debt Capital Markets with the organic storytelling of street performance provides a unique perspective on community engagement. Whether solo or in a duo, these moments in the city's "living rooms" are where I find the most authentic connections.
